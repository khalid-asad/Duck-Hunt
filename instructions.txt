Instructions:

The goal of the game is to press on as many ducks as you can with your mouse.
There is no time limit, but if you miss the duck 3 times, your score subtracts
by 100. If you click a duck, you get 100 points. If you want to search the 
high scores for a name press 1, sort the high scores from lowest to highest
score press 2, save your current score press 3. Once you get to 1000 score you
advance a round, same with 2500, 5000, 10000, etc until 1000000.

1 = search
2 = sort
3 = save
I = instructions
Q = change speed to fastest
W = change speed to medium
E = change speed to slowest
